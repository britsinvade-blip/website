Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our iOS apps

Are all your apps really free?

Yes! All Tayloriellos apps are completely free to download and use forever. There are no in-app purchases, subscriptions, or hidden fees. We believe great software should be accessible to everyone.

Do your apps contain ads?

No. None of our apps contain advertisements, pop-ups, or any form of intrusive marketing. We focus on creating clean, distraction-free experiences that let you focus on what matters.

What devices are your apps compatible with?

Our apps work on iPhone, iPad, and Mac (Apple Silicon). Most require iOS 17.0 or later. Each app is optimized for the specific device you're using, providing the best experience whether you're on a phone, tablet, or computer.

Can I use your apps offline?

Yes! Most of our apps work completely offline. Fire & Ladders, Tic-Tac-More!, and Lingotino don't require an internet connection. Spettle and Fastest Train need connectivity for receipt scanning and real-time train data respectively, but core features work offline.

How do I split a restaurant bill with Spettle?

Simply photograph your restaurant receipt. Spettle automatically extracts items and prices, then you assign each dish to the person who ordered it. The app calculates everyone's share including proportional tips and fees, then generates a shareable PDF summary.

Is Fastest Train accurate for UK train times?

Yes, Fastest Train uses official UK National Rail data to provide real-time departure information for nearly 3,000 stations across the UK. Platform information, delays, and cancellations are updated in real-time, ensuring you always have the most current information.

When will Lingotino be available?

Lingotino is scheduled to launch in December 2025. The app will feature over 1,000 Italian words, CEFR-aligned curriculum (A1-B2 levels), interactive games, and offline learning capabilities. You can sign up for early access notifications through our contact form.

How do I get support or report a bug?

You can contact us through our contact form. We typically respond within 24-48 hours. For urgent issues, please include your device model, iOS version, and a detailed description of the problem.
