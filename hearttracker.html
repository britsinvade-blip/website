Your Data, Your Business

Your health information is deeply personal. Most medical tracking apps force you to create an account, funneling your sensitive lipid and cholesterol data into corporate cloud servers.

We built Cholesterol Heart Tracker with an On-Device First architecture. Your data never leaves your phone. There are no accounts, no server uploads, and absolutely no data selling.

Clarity Over Clutter

Blood test results can be overwhelming. This app is designed to translate complex medical data into clean, readable trends. Log your HDL, LDL, and Triglycerides, and immediately see how your lifestyle changes are impacting your long-term health.

  • Private Storage: 100% of your data remains on your physical device.
  • Clear Trends: Visual charts that map your progress over time without the confusing jargon.
  • Doctor Ready: Easily view your history to share your exact numbers during your next GP visit.
  • No Accounts: Download the app and start logging your results immediately.
