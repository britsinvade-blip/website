How to Play

Master the game in minutes

1

Download & Open

Get Tic-Tac-More! from the App Store and launch the app. No sign-ups, no registration required.

2

Choose Your Mode

Select single-player to play against AI or multiplayer to challenge a friend on the same device.

3

Make Your Move

Tap any empty square to place your X or O. Try to get three in a row horizontally, vertically, or diagonally!

4

Win & Play Again

First to get three in a row wins! Tap "New Game" to play another round instantly.
