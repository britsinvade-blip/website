Fires & Ladders.

Kill time, save sanity. A beautifully simple reimagining of a board game classic, designed for the 5-minute gap in your day.

Download on the App Store

The Classic, Reimagined.

Some games don't need complex skill trees or daily login rewards; they just need to be fun. We took the iconic mechanics of Snakes & Ladders, swapped the reptiles for roaring fires, and wrapped it in a beautifully clean, modern iOS interface.

Zero Friction Fun

Mobile games today are exhausting. They demand you claim daily rewards, buy premium currency, and wait out artificial timers. Fire & Ladders respects your time. It loads instantly, plays beautifully, and lets you get on with your day.

  • Classic Mechanics: Roll the dice, climb the ladders, and hope you don't land on a fire. Pure luck, pure fun.
  • Local Multiplayer: No complicated online lobbies. Just pass your iPhone across the table and play with friends or family.
  • Stunning Minimalism: Clean, crisp aesthetics that look great in both Light and Dark mode, keeping the focus on the board.
  • No Accounts Needed: Open the app and you are instantly rolling the dice. Zero setup required.
