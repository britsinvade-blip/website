The Classic, Reimagined.

Some games don't need complex skill trees or daily login rewards; they just need to be fun. We took the iconic mechanics of Snakes & Ladders, swapped the reptiles for roaring fires, and wrapped it in a beautifully clean, modern iOS interface.

Zero Friction Fun

Mobile games today are exhausting. They demand you claim daily rewards, buy premium currency, and wait out artificial timers. Fires & Ladders respects your time. It loads instantly, plays beautifully, and lets you get on with your day.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Fires and Ladders?

Fires and Ladders is a free digital version of the classic snakes and ladders board game for iPhone. Instead of snakes, players avoid fires while climbing ladders to reach the finish. The game features modern design, chance cards, multiplayer support, and AI opponents.

Is Fires and Ladders free to play?

Yes, Fires and Ladders is completely free to download and play. The app is supported by discrete advertisements. There are no subscriptions, in-app purchases, or premium tiers.

How many players can play Fires and Ladders?

Fires and Ladders supports 1-6 players on a single device. You can play solo against AI opponents or gather friends and family for local multiplayer pass-and-play sessions. Perfect for game nights and family gatherings.

What are Chance Cards in Fires and Ladders?

Chance Cards are special game-changing bonuses that activate when you land on specific board spaces. They add strategic twists and surprises to the classic snakes and ladders gameplay, making each game unique and exciting.

Does Fires and Ladders require internet connection?

No, Fires and Ladders works completely offline. No internet connection required. All gameplay happens locally on your iPhone, making it perfect for flights, road trips, or anywhere without WiFi.

Can I play Fires and Ladders alone?

Yes! Fires and Ladders includes AI opponents with multiple difficulty levels. Practice your luck and strategy against computer players when friends aren't available. The AI provides challenging competition for solo gameplay.
