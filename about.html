The Tayloriellos Story

Who We Are

Tayloriellos is an independent, London-based app studio founded by Rob Taylor and Mario Picariello. As a husband-and-husband team, we combined our surnames to create the name Tayloriellos—a symbol of our shared passion project.

By day, we are senior tech professionals at Salesforce, building complex, enterprise-scale solutions. By night, Tayloriellos is our "Second Shift," a creative outlet where enterprise-level expertise meets everyday utility.

Our Mission

We are on a mission to reach 20,000 downloads in 2026 while proving that high-quality, human-centric software can be community-focused and free. We believe in stripping away the noise, the mandatory accounts, the subscriptions, and the data selling so you can focus on the human experience.

Our Core Values

Pro-Simplicity

We shift the narrative from "anti-subscription" to a positive focus on clean, focused utility. Our apps do exactly what they say they will, without the fluff.

Craftsmanship

By leveraging AI, our two-man studio produces the high-end craftsmanship and quality of a full-scale development team.

Community First

Every app we build is treated as a gift to the community. We are strictly committed to no ads and no data selling.

Privacy Stance

We enforce a strict "no accounts, no tracking" policy to ensure your absolute peace of mind.
