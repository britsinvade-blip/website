Frequently Asked Questions

How do I calculate the perfect ice cream recipe?

Download Gelato Wizard from the App Store. Start in Apprentice Mode with curated recipes and guided tutorials. Input your ingredients, and the app automatically calculates fat content, sugar levels, MSNF (milk solids non-fat), and water balance. Upgrade to Sorcerer Mode for advanced PAC/POD analysis and AI-powered ingredient recommendations.

What is PAC/POD analysis for gelato recipes?

PAC (Polysaccharides and Calcium) and POD (Proteins, Other solids, Dextrose equivalents) analysis is a professional method for balancing gelato recipes. Gelato Wizard's Sorcerer Mode automatically calculates these values, ensuring your recipe has the perfect texture, scoopability, and freeze point. This is the same analysis used by professional gelato makers.

Can I use this app for homemade ice cream recipes?

Absolutely! Gelato Wizard supports gelato, traditional ice cream, and sorbet recipes. The app includes product-specific target ranges for each type. Whether you're making rich Italian gelato or American-style ice cream, the recipe scoring adapts to your chosen category with appropriate fat and sugar targets.

Does the app have a sugar substitution calculator?

Yes! Gelato Wizard includes a comprehensive sugar substitution calculator. Replace table sugar with alternatives like honey, dextrose, or artificial sweeteners while maintaining proper sweetness and freeze point. The calculator automatically adjusts POD values and provides freeze point predictions for your modified recipe.

Do I need gelato-making experience to use this app?

No experience required! Apprentice Mode is designed for complete beginners with a 5-lesson course covering gelato science basics. The app explains concepts without overwhelming jargon. Start with curated recipes, then gradually customize as you learn. Advanced users can jump straight to Sorcerer Mode for professional recipe development.

Are my ice cream recipes saved privately?

Yes. All your custom gelato and ice cream recipes are stored directly on your iPhone with unlimited storage. Your recipes never sync to cloud servers. This ensures complete privacy for your proprietary formulas, perfect for home enthusiasts and professional makers alike.

Is Gelato Wizard free to use?

Gelato Wizard is free to download and use. The app is supported by advertising. All features including PAC/POD analysis, recipe scoring, sugar substitution calculator, batch scaling, and unlimited recipe storage are available without subscriptions or in-app purchases.
