This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and handle your information when you use our mobile applications.

Information We Collect

We are committed to protecting your privacy. Our apps may collect the following types of information:

Information you provide: When you use our apps, you may provide information such as photos, text, or other content depending on the app's functionality.

Automatically collected information: We may collect certain information automatically, including device type, operating system version, and app usage statistics to improve our services.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain our apps
  • Improve and optimize app performance
  • Respond to your requests and support needs
  • Send important updates about our apps

Data Storage and Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. Your data is stored securely and is only accessible by authorized personnel when necessary to provide our services.

Third-Party Services

Our apps may use third-party services that collect information used to identify you. These services have their own privacy policies addressing how they use such information.

FastestTrain retrieves live train and platform data from publicly available APIs such as:

  • National Rail Enquiries (UK)
  • Transport for London (TfL)

These services may collect minimal technical data when accessed. Please review their privacy policies for details.

Children's Privacy

Our apps are not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Data Retention

We retain your information only for as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information. You may also have the right to restrict or object to certain processing of your data. To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information below.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us via our online form.
