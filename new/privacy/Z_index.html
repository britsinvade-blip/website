Absolute Privacy.

We believe that high-quality, human-centric software doesn't have to harvest your data. Here is our straightforward privacy promise.

1. On-Device First

For apps like Cholesterol Heart Tracker and Spettle, your personal data never leaves your phone. We do not maintain databases of your health records, your receipts, or your dining habits. All processing and saving happen strictly on your local hardware.

2. Zero Forced Accounts

We hate forced logins just as much as you do. None of our apps require you to create an account, hand over an email address, or link a social profile to access their core features.

3. How We Keep the Lights On

To fund development while remaining independent, some of our free apps (like Tic-Tac-More! and Fire & Ladders) include minimal, embedded advertisements. These are served through standard networks that may collect anonymous usage data to serve relevant ads, but we strictly configure them to respect your tracking preferences and Apple's App Tracking Transparency rules.

4. This Website

If you are browsing tayloriellos.co.uk, we use industry-standard tools (like Google Analytics) to understand how many people visit us. We use a consent management platform (Cookiebot) to ensure we only track what you explicitly allow. We don't sell this data to data brokers.

5. Get in Touch

We are humans, not algorithms. If you have any questions about how your data is handled, or if you need to request the removal of any analytics data, we are always available.
